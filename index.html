Bridge the Gender Technology Gap

ABOUT US

W 4Tech is designed to bridge the gender technology gap by helping women access training and educational opportunities in areas of Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) and Information Technology (IT) through meetups, workshops, seminars and conferences. Aimed at building digital literacy in women, expose them to available employment and entrepreneurship opportunities within the technology space.

WHAT I NEED TO KNOW.

Women 4Tech is a club born out to create awareness, encourage and advance women in technology from classroom to the boardroom, to improve their knowledge in Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) and Information Communication Technology (ICT) as well reach out to factual information.

To increase the number of women that are actively involved in STEM fields as well as ensure that the digital gap is bridged between male and female within the technology ecosystem.

MEMBERSHIP AND REGISTRATION.

The club is meant to develop our community in order to increase the chance of career opportunities and empowering women into technology.

Membership of this club is open to all women irrespective of their culture, status or religious belief.

To provide Leadership / Teamwork Skills and develop brighter and better IT Personals(women) at the end of the year.

ACTIVITIES

Building Capacity

W4Tech provides general interactive session based on IT, sharing more light, discussing and solving problems within the community and also provide the conceptual approach to social, behavioral change and leads to infrastructure development which will strengthening the skill, competencies and abilities of women in technology.

Programming Development

At the heart of any tech product or digital service is coding. W4Tech shall covers the programming development in PHP, PYTHON, JAVA, KOTLIN, JSON, Frameworks e.t.c and side technologies like: Cloud Computing, Cryptography e.t.c

Digital Skills Training

Training women to have the ability to find, evaluate, utilize, share and create content using information technology and internet, the digital age is expanding into all areas of our lives and it is not just those male in the IT that will need to be alert of this change.

HangOuts

Women 4Tech provides opportunity for individual-Mentor interactive section where we all meet to talk on general base on things happening around the world, get to discover yourself and people around your in a socialized way..
